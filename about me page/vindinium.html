What is Vindinium?

      Vindinium is a role playing game.
      In which players pick a character name and are assigned a key. Which is just a series of numbers and letters.
      The players are given a base javascript for AI that players build off of.
      To build off of it means that they will assign new tasks in the javascript which will apply to their characters AI in the game.
      The characters in the game are then able to complete the tasks that have been assigned.
      The game consists of players taking mines for gold, stealing other players mines, and characters fighting eachother.
      To win the game one player must acummulate more gold than all other players.
      While a game is happening players can watch the screen or their terminal which shows all of the moves and tasks carried.
