What is Vindinium?

      Vindinium is a role playing game.
      In which players pick a character name and are assigned a key. Which is just a series of numbers and letters
      The players are given a base javascript that they build off.
      To build off of it means that they will assign new tasks in the javascript which will apply to their characters AI in the game.
      The characters in the game are then able to complete the tasks that have been assigned.
