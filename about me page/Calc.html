Calculator pro is a beautifully created drop down calculator. That uses addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, to the power of, percentage, and Pi. This calculator was made in brief period of about 4-5 hours. (Results may vary).Buttons for decoration only. Variables such as pi and precent conversion only work when a number is input into the first slot and not the second. The number in the second slot will be ignored.
